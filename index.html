Rajasekaran
Trust Founder

The Shri Makara Jyothi Trust aims to create a society where spirituality brings people together, fostering peace and unity. We help individuals grow personally and spiritually, teaching values like discipline and resilience. We also work to preserve our cultural heritage, celebrating traditions that connect us and enrich our lives.

Through educational programs and awareness campaigns, we support people on their journey to self-improvement. We believe in the power of community and collective action, organizing volunteer opportunities and service projects. By coming together, we can make a positive impact on our communities and spread kindness and compassion.

Our mission is rooted in sincerity and empathy, striving to create a world where everyone feels supported on their spiritual path. We celebrate cultural diversity and aim to empower individuals to lead fulfilling lives guided by spiritual values. The Shri Makara Jyothi Trust is committed to making a meaningful difference in the world, one act of kindness at a time.

  • Ayyappan Temple construction



    The construction of the Ayyappan Temple is set to begin in January 21st, 2024, and we're currently seeking donations. Your support is essential to this project. It's a collaborative effort, and every contribution, regardless of its size, plays a crucial role in bringing our shared vision to life.

  • Annadanam in the Brihadisvara Temple


    Each year, we come together to cook and serve nutritious meals to over 20,000 devotees, turning this occasion into a joyous celebration of communal harmony and shared blessings. This monumental task is not only a privilege but also a responsibility that we uphold with dedication and gratitude.

    This is made possible through the generous financial support from GS Foundation, K.S Trust, and Shri Makara Jyothi Trust. Their commitment empowers us to extend our outreach and make a more significant impact on the lives of those we serve. We extend our heartfelt appreciation to the numerous like-minded individuals who volunteer their time and efforts to make this event a success.

In 1986,

guided by the teachings of Mani Gurunathar, Police Gurunathar, and Nagasubramanian Gurunathar, I undertook the Sabari Yatra. Organized by Swami Paravidhyanandha Saraswathi, this pilgrimage left me with cherished memories and profound experiences, revealing the true essence of Bakthi, devotion, and human service.

Inspired by the transformative impact of service on both giver and receiver, I believe in the power of collaboration to influence others positively.


This belief led to the establishment of Shri Makara Jyothi Trust, extending our vision beyond religious enrichment to initiatives like Annadhanam, education support, medical aid, and community service.

Join us in this journey of compassion, unity, and purpose. Let's contribute to a harmonious society where the light of service illuminates every heart.

With gratitude and devotion,
Shri. S. Rajasekaran
